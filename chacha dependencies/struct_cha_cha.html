Detailed Description

ChaCha stream cipher.

This is Bernstein and ECRYPT's ChaCha. It is _slightly_ different from the IETF's version of ChaCha called ChaChaTLS.

See also
ChaCha, a variant of Salsa20 (2008.01.28).
